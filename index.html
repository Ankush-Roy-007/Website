About Us




Just like robotics in itself has very small beginnings, we too had a very small start. The start was so small indeed that at that time nobody could have predicted what this club would turn out into the future.

The journey of the Robotics Club started with working on bots that could just move around, today we have bots that can move around, pick around and even shoot around. Earlier we had only mechanical projects populating the wall of fame today we have projects from the Embedded, Image Processing, Artificial Intelligence, you name it and we have it.

The tale of the Robotics club is a perfect blend of the stories of each of its members, they too started small but today they have such complex contributions to the club, that it is difficult to imagine how they had started.

This is the beauty of the work at the robotics club, we are not there to prove something, we are just there to enjoy in the making and be a part of something that is very special, a feeling that only the true members of the robotics club can relish.
